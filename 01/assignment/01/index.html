EPIK HIGH

about.

EPIK HIGH is a Korean Hip-Hop band, composed of Tablo, Mithra Jin, and DJ Tukutz.

They debuted with an album 'Map of the Human Soul' in 2003, and they gained mainstream success with their 2005 album, 'Swan Songs.'

Their most recent album, 'We've Done Something Wonderful' is the first 9th full-length album in Korean Hip-Hop history.
